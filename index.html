Mission

To bring hackathons to places that don't have them. By energizing local tech communities, and empowering organizers to create events by giving them the building blocks and the support they need to do so.

Vision

Allow anyone, anywhere, to create a great, simple, low-cost hackathon.

Values

  • People over Production
  • Teachers over Trinkets
  • Participation over Prizes

What is FrontierHack?

FrontierHack partners with organizers in communities that don’t have hackathons to bring the experience to everyone. We provide the necessary resources and expertise to put on these events while utilizing the organizer’s knowledge and connections to make the best event possible.

FrontierHack is committed to putting on low-cost hackathons, with mentors who are dedicated to providing support to the community. We throw away the assumption that hackathons need to be giant productions and focus instead on creating an environment where developers can thrive.
